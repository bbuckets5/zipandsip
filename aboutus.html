S & S Zip and Sip Natural Drinks & Teas is a family-owned small business in the Bahamas that provides only the finest and freshest juices using ingredients sourced from our local farmers, farmers markets, exchange, and stores.

From hand-picking leafy greens to selecting organic fruits and vegetables, we are committed to offering our customers high-quality products and exceptional service.

Our cold-pressed technique produces nutrient-rich juices that are not only superior in taste but also a smooth texture. We proudly serve customers across the Bahamas, as well as visitors from around the world.

We are committed to providing every one of our customers with premium and sustainable drinks, by ensuring that our reusable bottles are BPA- and PET-friendly: meaning that they are free of toxins and other harmful cancer-causing agents.

We want to say thank you in advance for your patronage and continued support of our small business. We look forward to serving you for many more years to come as we grow together in the betterment of creating a healthier body one juice at a time.
